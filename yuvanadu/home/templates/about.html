About Us

The Harish Group is a diversified company that provides supply of building materials, manufactures concrete blocks, and operates a civil construction contracting business.

Guided by a longstanding legacy of trust, the company's operations are rooted in reliability and integrity.

Chit services are the first financial product offered by the Harish Group. Our objective is to deliver supplementary Yuvanadu Chits Services designed to meet the unique savings and borrowing needs of our clients.

Prudent financial planning involves both saving money and having access to credit. Individuals may need to take out loans to purchase assets or cover unexpected expenses. Maintaining a balanced approach with savings and borrowing options can help ensure financial stability throughout one's lifetime.

Our chit fund, a unique savings and loan scheme, caters to customers across all classes. Simultaneously, we uphold the highest standards of ethics, best practices, professional integrity, corporate governance, and regulatory compliance.

Established in 2024, the Harish Group's inaugural chit fund was built on a foundation of ethics, trust, and financial responsibility. Drawing on our extensive financial market expertise, we are well-equipped to navigate challenges and seize emerging opportunities. That same year, we launched our Chit financial services business in a new form by incorporating a new company called Yuvanadu Chits Pvt Ltd.

The HG brand is renowned for its trustworthiness and integrity. We offer a diverse portfolio of Yuvanadu Chits products and schemes that customers can conveniently access through our extensive network of branch, agents, and online channels. Additionally, our members have the flexibility to choose from multiple security options for their prize money payouts - a feature that sets us apart from traditional chit fund operators.
